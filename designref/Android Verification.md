# Privacy Policy

**1\. Introduction**

Welcome to Scotty, provided by Estatech Co, Ltd. We are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Scotty mobile application and our smart door lock ecosystem.

**2\. Information We Collect & Device Permissions**

To provide seamless smart home automation and door lock functionality, the Scotty app requires access to the following device features:

* Bluetooth & Device Connectivity: We require Bluetooth access to discover, connect to, and operate your smart door locks.  
* Location Services (Older Devices Only): For users on Android 11 and older, the Android operating system requires "Fine Location" access to scan for Bluetooth devices. We do not use this permission to track your physical GPS whereabouts.  
* Background Processing: The app runs a background service and requests to bypass battery optimizations to ensure continuous, reliable connection to your smart devices and to deliver real-time push notifications.  
* Account Data: When you register, we collect basic information such as your name, email, and password, which is securely transmitted over the Internet to our servers.

**3\. How We Use Your Information**

We use the collected information solely to:

*  Operate and authenticate your smart door lock.  
*  Send you critical push notifications (e.g., lock status, low battery alerts).  
*  Maintain and improve our smart home infrastructure.

**4\. Data Retention and Account Deletion**

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. You can delete your account directly within the Scotty app via the Personal  Account menu. Upon request, we will remove your personal data from our active databases, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

**5\. Contact Us**
